We builds solutions by empowering teritary institutions' students with financial aid
through loans with low interest rates and at affordable prices.

KESA was started in 2022 by a group of people from different financial sector backgrounds who shared a common dream:
one of challenging the status quo. At the core of KESA, we empower our clients which are students of all academic institutions
to help save, invest and most importantly acquire loans for their academic pursuits. However, the students are our greatest asset and at KESA, we help build tomorrow's leaders.

KESA provides supply chain finance and funding solutions to students who find it difficult to fund their academic activities.
Our fintech solution are capable of saving, investing and lending with transparency, convenience and choice as our founding
principles. we have designed real-times, online solutions that make savings, investing and lending transparent, simple and
seamless to execute.
